Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United Stated?

Tacos first appeared in the United States around 1905, introduced by Mexican migrants who brought the food with them as they worked in the railroads and mines. These early tacos were primarily street food sold from carts, with mentions in newspapers and integration into the local culture of the American Southwest from 1905. (Source: smithsonian Magazine
